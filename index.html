The shift

Revenue teams have enough dashboards. They need more leverage.

For two decades, revenue management software has helped hotels see the work: demand shifts, competitor movement, booking pace, channel performance and forecast risk.

But visibility is no longer the bottleneck. The bottleneck is the manual work between insight and action.

BEONx is moving that work into supervised AI agents.
